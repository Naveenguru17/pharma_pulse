M01AE - Propionic Acid

Derivatives (NSAIDs)

Common Drugs: Ibuprofen, Naproxen

Uses: Commonly used to alleviate pain, reduce fever, and treat inflammation associated with conditions like arthritis, menstrual pain, and minor injuries. These drugs block the production of prostaglandins to reduce pain and inflammation.

Side Effects: Gastrointestinal issues (e.g., ulcers, bleeding), kidney problems, increased risk of cardiovascular events.
