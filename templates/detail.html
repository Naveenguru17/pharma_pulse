Dataset Overview

Our dataset is a comprehensive collection of pharmaceutical sales data, capturing detailed daily records over several years. It includes 2,106 observations across 13 variables. Each entry represents a specific date and provides insights into the sales of various pharmaceutical products, categorized by their ATC (Anatomical Therapeutic Chemical) classification codes.
