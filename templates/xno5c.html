N05C - Hypnotics and Sedatives

Common Drugs: Zolpidem (Ambien), Temazepam (Restoril).

Uses: These drugs are used to induce sleep in individuals with insomnia or to sedate patients before surgeries. They work by slowing down brain activity, leading to relaxation and sleep.

Side Effects: Dependency, drowsiness, dizziness, memory problems, potential for misuse.
